Over the course of 2024, I spent lots of time working on this musical side project: my very first album. This whole project was composed, performed, recorded and mixed by me, in my basement "studio." I recorded and mixed everything in Ableton Live Lite. Some of the biggest challanges for me was playing guitar without any experience on 'One More Lap', playing saxophone for the fisrt time since highschool on 'FOCUS' and 'Aurora', and recording and mixing the drums with 5 microphones and no recording knowledge. The biggest challenge of all though, was learning how to balance life and work for such a big project done all by myself. This album was defintely a worthy learning experience for so many musical, audio, and life skills. While I wait for this album to be distributed, here are some demos for 5 of the tracks. Please enjoy 'Creative Liberty'!
